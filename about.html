About Johar InfraTech

Who We Are

Johar InfraTech is a forward-looking Indian MSME delivering decentralized computing infrastructure for AI, ML, and Web3 technologies. We operate and maintain nodes for networks such as Gensyn, Aztec, Drosera, and more.

Our Mission

To make high-performance computing accessible for everyone and to support global innovation through decentralized networks and infrastructure services.

Our Vision

We envision a decentralized future powered by trustless computation, scalable infrastructure, and inclusive technology that empowers every innovator globally.
